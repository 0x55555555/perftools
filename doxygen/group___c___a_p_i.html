PERF_C_EXPORT const perf_identity* perf_find_identity ( perf_config *  ctx)

Find the identity of the context.

The identity describes the machine hardware, and is used in graphing to identify threads of tests.

Note
The identity memory is managed by the config, it should not be freed.
